Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 31, 2026

Smile on Staten Island (smileonstatenisland.com), operated by Staten Island Cosmetic Dentist (Pieroni & Fiorello D.D.S.), located at 763 Clove Road, Staten Island, NY 10310 ("we," "us"), provides this website to share information about our practice and let visitors get in touch. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

Information we collect

When you submit our contact/appointment-request form, we collect the information you provide — such as your name, email address, phone number, preferred day/time, reason for your visit, and message.

Please do not submit sensitive medical information through this form. It is for general inquiries and appointment requests only and is not a secure or confidential channel for health details. We will collect any necessary health information directly and securely at your appointment.

How we use it

We use this information solely to respond to your inquiry and schedule or follow up on your request. We do not sell, rent, or trade your information.

How submissions are handled

Our form is processed by Formspree, a third-party form service that delivers your submission to us. Formspree handles the data under its own privacy policy.

Analytics and cookies

This website does not use tracking cookies. If we use analytics, it is a privacy-friendly, cookieless tool that collects only aggregate, anonymized traffic data and does not identify you. See our Cookie Policy for details.

Data retention

We keep submissions only as long as needed to respond and maintain appropriate records.

Your choices

You may request deletion of the information you submitted by using the contact form on our website or by calling us at 718-442-5319.

Changes

We may update this policy; the "last updated" date reflects the latest version.

Contact

Questions: use the contact form on our website or call us at 718-442-5319.
