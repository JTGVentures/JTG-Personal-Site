First Visit?

Here's what to expect.

Plan on about an hour. A friendly intake, a thorough exam with the doctor (no rushing), digital imaging if needed, and a clear, no-pressure conversation about next steps. Bring your ID, insurance card if you have one, and any X-rays or records from a previous dentist.
